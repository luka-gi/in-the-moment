Are you fed up with setting a thousand reminders a day like us?

In The Moment is going to change that.


For students, workers, and forgetful people who need to balance the stress of the day with peace of mind, In The Moment is a reminder app that uses your location unlike the regular reminders on your phone. This app will only notifiy you when you get to the certain area where you set your reminders instead of just relying on the time. Now, your pesky "helpful" device won't get in the way of enjoying your day! A life so sublime, I even made it rhyme!


Tired of eating the same old food at your favorite restaurant?

Well, using the In The Moment app, you can remind your habit-loving future self to get that mouth-watering menu choice next time!


Or perhaps you need to keep yourself on track at work or school.

Enjoy a calm, peaceful morning on the drive there. (Or jamming out ...that's the way I like to do it...)
