Landon, Marijuana User

Landon is a new Computer Science graduate from the University of Louisiana Lafayette and specializes in web development. He is a Louisiana-native, has never left the state in all of his life, and wants to make a higher wage than jobs he would get locally. He finds and gets accepted into his dream job as a web developer in Seattle, Washington. After working on-site at his new job for a few days, he walks past a marijuana dispensary on his way back home. Since he has never tried any recreational drugs, he decides to walk in, buy some marijuana, and soon becomes a daily smoker.

While on his "highs", he likes to walk around the beautiful city of Seattle at night. He is still responsible with recreational marijuana, but sometimes forgets which vending machines around his area has his favorite snacks. Since Landon has a background in developing technologies, he thinks there must be an app to remind him while he is under the influence.

Scenarios

Stories

  1. While resting in bed one night, Landon discovers the In The Moment app and decides to try it out the next day. In the morning, Landon's supervisors tell him he needs to learn a new technology to work on the current project; he spends all the next few hours getting frustrated with it since this is something completely new to him. On his lunch break, he opens up In The Moment and creates a few reminder pins he will visit that night after his stressful day.

    On his nightly walk, he sees a homeless person sitting on the sidewalk. Landon talks to this man about his life on the streets, his overall situation, and how he ended up there. Feeling a bond between this man and himself, Landon offers to share his marijuana with the homeless man to let him take his mind off of the worries of the world for a moment. As the two are smoking and walking down the street, Landon gets a reminder on his phone that they are approaching a vending machine that has been pinned by Landon earlier that day and both men share a bag of Doritoes.

  2. Landon, now parted ways with the homeless man, walks down a couple of blocks and sees a crowded McDonald's. He knows he'll be waiting for a least 20 minutes but decides to walk in since he doesn't have anywhere to be anyway. After waiting in line, he orders his usual two Hot-n-Spicy's and sits down in a booth. Since he came by himself, he looks around at people and makes jokes to himself at what they are saying to each other. His eyes then drift to the menu again and he sees a new item on the menu that he hasn't seen before: the Shamrock shake. He wants to get it but he already spent his money so he takes a picture of the shake, creates a reminder on the In The Moment app, and attaches the image to it. Next time he comes to his local McDonald's, he will make sure to get the Shamrock shake instead of his two Hot-n-Spicy's.

  1. As a marijuana user, I want to be able to remember the where some vending machines are so I can buy my favorite snacks.

  2. As someone who eats fast food, I want to remember the menu so I can buy new items instead of my usual ones.
